About us

There is truly something to be found for even the most discerning of readers.
Our friendly and knowledgeable booksellers are more than happy to assist you in exploring our shop and taking advantage of the many services we offer.

These include our diverse and relaxed events programme, from Hillary Clinton to small launches and readings with the Royal Academy, as well as our complimentary gift-wrapping service and competitive worldwide shipping.

We also offer a selection of beautiful first edition books for something a little different, and are more than happy to try and source any individual requests.

FAQs

Do you also sell second-hand books?

Yes, you will find a dedicated section near the checkouts, at competitive prices

Can I bring you some old books I don't need anymore?

We would be glad to accept your books and give you a small payment, given that the books are in decent condition

Are your events free?

It depends, many of the meetings and conferences we offer are free, but there are some workshops that require a small participation payment. Check the single event's page if you have any doubt.
